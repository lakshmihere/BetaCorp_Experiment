Experiment: Peer-Mentor & Leadership Endorsement Improves Trust

BetaCorp is testing whether combining peer mentorship, leadership endorsement, and micro-literacy training can shift moderate/hesitant GenAI users from cautious experimentation to confident, high-value usage.

Experiment Blueprint
(i) Experiment Title

Peer-Mentor, Leadership Endorsement improves Trust

(ii) Hypothesis

Pairing moderate/hesitant users with peer mentors and visible leadership endorsement, while providing structured micro-literacy boosts, will increase confidence, trust, and usage frequency by at least 20% compared to similar users who do not receive these social-support interventions, because VOC shows these groups struggle with uncertainty and trust.

(iii) Team-level A/B Experiment
Treatment Group – Group A High-support condition
  • Peer mentorship (GenAI guidance)
  • Leadership endorsement (GenAI policy & guidelines)
  • Quick GenAI literacy training
  • GenAI strategic workflow training
  • Social proof + clear permission + skill → maximum adoption shift.
Control Group – Group B Baseline condition
  • Continues normal self-directed learning
  • No structured peer mentorship
  • No explicit leadership endorsement intervention
(iv) Target Audience – Moderate / Hesitant Users
  • Confidence score = 2 or 3 (on a 1–5 scale)
  • Use GenAI occasionally, not regularly
  • Report governance uncertainty and fear of “getting it wrong”
(v) Procedure – Step-by-Step
  1. Send baseline survey (confidence + trust + usage).
  2. Run micro-literacy training.
  3. Assign peer mentors.
  4. Leadership issues endorsement message.
  5. Run GenAI Strategic Workflow Integration training.
  6. Collect usage analytics and qualitative comments.
  7. Run post-intervention survey.
(vi) Success Metrics
  • Confidence: Treatment outperforms Control by +20–30% uplift.
  • Usage Frequency: 2× increase in weekly GenAI usage (Treatment).
  • Trust in AI: +25% increase in governance & reliability scores (Treatment).
  • Workflow Integration: +20% increase in integrated tool usage (Treatment).
(vii) Decision Criteria – Color-coded
Scale the Intervention (Green)
  • ≥ 20% uplift in confidence.
  • ≥ 25% increase in weekly usage.
  • Leadership message correlates with usage & trust spike.
  • Qualitative comments show improved trust & clarity.
Stop / Redesign (Red)
  • < 10% movement in key metrics.
  • Low participation or engagement.
  • No clear behavioral change in logs or workflows.
(viii) Next Steps

Build and iterate on a dashboard UI that monitors the human side of GenAI adoption at BetaCorp and provides explainable AI (XAI)-style insights about trust, literacy, and leadership impact.
